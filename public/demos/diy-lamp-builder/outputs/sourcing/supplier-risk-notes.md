# Supplier Risk Notes

| Risk | What to check | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| CRI overclaim | Compare color rendering on printed book pages | Buy one extra LED candidate |
| Wrong voltage | Confirm strip, dimmer, adapter are all 24V | Do not mix 5V/12V/24V |
| Strip too wide | Check aluminum channel inner width | Buy channel+diffuser as set |
| Flicker | Phone slow-motion test | Try second dimmer |
| Diffuser not milky enough | LED dots visible | Use deeper channel or opal diffuser |
| Adapter connector mismatch | Plug does not fit dimmer/strip | Plan solder or adapter plug |
| Heat buildup | Shell gets hot near LED | Improve aluminum path or reduce power |
| Shipping delay | Overseas supplier takes too long | Prefer local channel for first sample |
