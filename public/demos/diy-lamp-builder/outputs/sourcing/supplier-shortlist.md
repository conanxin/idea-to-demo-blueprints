# Supplier Shortlist

## LED strip candidates

1. SuperBrightLEDs / Industrial Commercial Lighting style high-CRI COB kits
   - Good for first prototype because kit-style listings reduce wiring ambiguity.
   - Check exact strip width, cut interval, voltage, and included controller.

2. Armacost RibbonFlex Pro 24V COB / tunable white
   - Good if you want a more recognizable home-improvement retail channel.
   - Tunable white is powerful but adds controller complexity.

3. Low-cost marketplace COB 24V strips
   - Useful as visual mock or backup comparison.
   - Do not trust CRI/current/thermal claims without testing.

## Aluminum channel candidates

1. 1m aluminum profile with opal cover
   - Good baseline for cutting to 240-260mm lamp head.
   - Confirm inner width and diffuser type.

2. V-shape / angled aluminum profile
   - Good for downward beam angle.
   - May require CAD slot adjustment.
3. 12mm diffuser channel
   - Good if LED strip width is 10-12mm.
   - Test diffuser depth to reduce LED hotspots.

## Power / dimmer candidates

- 24V 1A-2A adapter is enough for a short reading-lamp prototype.
- Inline PWM dimmer is easy to test.
- Touch dimmer is nicer for product feel but may require enclosure design.
